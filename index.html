Based in Fulham, West London, Chelsea Football Club is a professional football club from England. The team was founded on March 10th, 1905 at The Rising Sun pub. Their home games take place at Stamford Bridge, and they compete in the Premier League, which is the highest tier of English football. In 1955, the club won its first significant title, the League championship. The FA Cup was won by the club for the first time in 1970, followed by their first European title, the Cup Winners' Cup, in 1971. Finally, in 2022, they became the third English club to win the Club World Cup.
